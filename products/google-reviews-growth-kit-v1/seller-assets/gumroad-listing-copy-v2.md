## Listing body (paste this into the description field)

```
Most med spas do not have a Google reviews problem because clients are unhappy. They have a Google reviews workflow problem. The asks happen inconsistently, follow-ups fall through, and replies happen whenever someone remembers.

This kit replaces the chaos with a 30 minute weekly system the front desk can actually run.

What you get

The Google Reviews Growth Kit is a single Excel workbook (also works in Google Sheets and Numbers) with everything organized in tabs:

- Review Tracker: who you asked, when, who responded, who needs a 7 day follow-up
- Dashboard: this week's asks, replies, unanswered reviews, and service-recovery flags
- Copy Builder: drop in a client name and treatment, get a ready-to-send SMS or email
- Reply Templates: starter language for positive, mixed, and negative reviews (privacy-aware)
- AI prompt library: short, specific prompts for drafting replies in ChatGPT or Claude
- Policy-safe review request guide: what to say, what not to say, what Google's policy actually prohibits
- Service recovery workflow: when to move a review private and how to handle the conversation
- Quickstart and Start Here guides so the front desk can run this without you

What this kit is not

- Not automation software. It does not send the messages for you. It is a workbook and template pack.
- Not legal or HIPAA advice. Templates are written to avoid clinical-detail exposure but your compliance officer is the final word.
- Not a paid software subscription. No login, no AI API key, no monthly fee. Buy once, use forever.

Who this is for

- Med spa owners with one location or a small front desk team
- Aesthetic clinics adding a reputation workflow for the first time
- Solo injectors and small clinics that need a structured ask process but can not justify a $200/month review platform
- Office managers who need to delegate review work without losing visibility

Format

One ZIP file. Instant download after purchase.

Want a smaller starting point?

The $10 Med Spa Review Reply Swipe File is the reply-templates piece on its own. If your only problem right now is what to write, start there.

https://localliftstudio.gumroad.com/l/dxppjd

Honest note on policy

This kit is built around honest review requests, not review gating. Do not offer incentives for reviews. Do not ask only satisfied clients. Do not ask for a specific rating. Both Google and the FTC have rules against incentivized reviews and the penalty is severe.
```
