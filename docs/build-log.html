How this site gets built.

A running, dated log of what gets shipped at Local Lift Studio. Honest about what an AI agent generated, what a human decided, and what made it out to the public site.

Most of the writing, code, and assets here are produced by an AI agent (Claude) working under instructions from a small team. The agent picks niches, drafts content, builds the products, generates pin assets, writes the site code, and pushes commits to GitHub Pages. A human reviews taste, validates accuracy with working professionals, and clicks the publish step on third-party platforms where APIs are not available.

This page exists because if you bought something here or read an article and felt it was useful, you might be curious how it was actually made. The short version: a long-form agent ran the build, and a person made the calls on what to ship.

2026-05-11

Notary toolkit validated. Dental swipe file built. Site SEO doubled.

The validation cycle for a separate Notary Signing Agent toolkit closed with two substantive corrections from working notaries on r/Notary. The Right to Cancel handling was wrong in the original draft (signed copies go back to the title company, not to the borrower) and was fixed within an hour. A second product, the Dental Practice Review Reply Swipe File, was built end to end including 35 HIPAA-aware templates, a $10 price point, and a landing page. The site gained 5 new long-tail SEO articles, a free negative-review reply generator tool, and structured data on every product page. Every Local Lift article now offers a $10 entry alongside the $29 kit so cold visitors are not asked to make a $29 leap from a zero-review listing.

2026-05-09 to 2026-05-10

$10 funnel goes live. CTA expansion shipped.

The Med Spa Review Reply Swipe File was published as a $10 tripwire on Gumroad. The tripwire landing page had been sitting with a placeholder mailto link for days, sending every interested buyer to compose an email instead of a checkout page. Replacing the link took 5 minutes; building the $10 entry point everywhere else on the site (six articles, the home page navigation) took an hour. The funnel is now consistent end to end.

2026-05-05 to 2026-05-08

First product live. First outreach round. First reply.

The Google Reviews Growth Kit for Med Spas launched on Gumroad at $29. The first cold outreach round reached 20 med spa owners with a single research question. One spa owner replied with one sentence ("we don't really track them") which became the first real data point about whether the product solves a real problem.

What this is not

  • Not an autonomous business in the sci-fi sense. A human is still the operator and the owner.
  • Not a stunt. Every product on this site is real and the templates inside have been peer-reviewed by working professionals where applicable.
  • Not anti-human. The AI does the drafting and the building. Humans do the trust, the taste, and the customer conversation.
Why we are doing this in public.

An AI tool gets a lot of credit for capability that does not actually exist yet. We wanted to find out, by building real products and selling them to real people, what the agent can and cannot do without a human in the loop. The honest answer is in the build log above and in the products themselves.

If a tool or template you bought here was useful, that is the answer. If it was not, that is also the answer. Either way the log keeps running.
