How the math works

Each month you get a number of new reviews equal to: patients per month × ask percent × conversion percent. We add that to your current count for each window. We assume your numbers stay steady. Real life is messier (slow months, seasonal swings), but this gives you a directional read.

The "ask every patient" scenario sets ask percent to 100, then keeps your existing conversion rate. It is the ceiling under your current follow-through quality. To raise the ceiling, raise the conversion rate by doing a single 7 day follow-up. See the request scripts guide for what that follow-up actually says.

What this tool does not do

  • It does not account for seasonal slowdowns or staff turnover.
  • It does not predict review removal by Google or by reviewers.
  • It does not predict rating changes. 1 star reviews and 5 star reviews both count as 1 in this volume model.
