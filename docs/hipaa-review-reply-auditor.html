What the audit catches

  • Words that confirm the reviewer was a patient (their name, "your appointment," "your visit," "thanks for coming in")
  • Treatment names (Botox, filler, laser, peel, microneedling, etc.)
  • Provider names with titles (Dr., Nurse, NP, RN, PA)
  • Appointment specifics (date references, day of the week, time)
  • Billing or insurance terms (refund, charge, copay, deductible)
  • Length warnings (replies over 5 sentences read defensive)

What it does not catch: subtle context clues, voice tone, state-specific board rules. This is a first-pass scan. Your compliance officer is the final word.
