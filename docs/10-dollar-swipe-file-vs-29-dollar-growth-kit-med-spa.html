The honest decision tree

Get the $10 swipe file if

  • You already get enough Google reviews. You just need to reply better.
  • Your front desk is the one writing replies and you want them to stop guessing.
  • You want a quick, low-risk first purchase to see if the brand delivers before spending $29.
  • You're a solo injector or single-room spa where a tracker would be overkill.

Get the $29 growth kit if

  • Your review count has been stuck at the same number for months.
  • You know you should be asking patients for reviews but you keep forgetting who you asked and when.
  • You have a front desk lead and you want them to run a weekly review workflow without you in the loop.
  • You manage more than one location or more than one provider and you need a shared tracker.
  • You're already asking for reviews informally and you want to systematize it.

What is NOT in either product

  • Neither product offers review removal services. Google's policy decides what comes down and when.
  • Neither product is legal or HIPAA advice. The templates are written to avoid exposing protected health information, but your practice's compliance officer is the final word.
  • Neither product incentivizes reviews with discounts or freebies. That is against Google's policy and can wipe your listing.
  • Neither product automates review responses. A human reviews and posts every reply.

Frequently asked questions

Should I start with the $10 swipe file or the $29 growth kit?

Start with the $10 swipe file if your only problem right now is what to write in a reply. Start with the $29 growth kit if your problem is that nobody is asking for reviews in the first place or you don't know who is overdue for a follow-up.

Is the $29 kit just the $10 swipe file plus extras?

The $29 kit includes the reply templates from the $10 swipe file. It also adds a request tracker, a weekly workflow checklist, a copy builder for fresh requests, and a setup guide. The $10 swipe file is reply-only; the $29 kit is the full system.

Can I buy the swipe file first and upgrade later?

Yes. Buy the $10 swipe file first if you're not sure. If you find yourself wanting a tracker and a weekly workflow, the $29 kit is a separate purchase. There's no current automatic upgrade path.

Are both privacy-aware and policy-safe?

Yes. Both products avoid confirming patient status or clinical details in public replies. Neither incentivizes reviews with discounts or perks, which violates Google's policy.

Which one helps me grow review volume faster?

The $29 kit, because it covers the asking side (request templates, tracker, weekly workflow). The $10 swipe file is the reply side only. Replies don't grow your review count; requests do.
