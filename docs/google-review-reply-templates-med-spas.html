Med spa reputation workflow - Reply examples

Google review reply templates for med spas.

Use these examples to respond to Google reviews professionally without revealing client treatment details, sounding defensive, or promising outcomes.

The full kit pairs reply templates with a tracker, dashboard, request message builder, and weekly SOP so reviews do not become a random front-desk scramble.

Review replies are public trust signals. A future med spa client may read them before booking a facial, injectable consultation, laser service, body treatment, or skin consultation.

The goal is not to sound clever. The goal is to acknowledge the reviewer, stay privacy-safe, and show future clients that your team is calm and professional.

Privacy-safe reminder: do not confirm private treatment details, medical history, appointment specifics, or client identity beyond what the reviewer already made public. Keep replies general and respectful.

Positive Google Review Reply Template

Use this for a warm review that mentions a good experience.

Thank you for taking the time to share this. We are so glad you had a positive experience with our team at [Med Spa Name]. We appreciate your feedback and look forward to welcoming you again.

Why it works: it thanks the client without repeating specific treatment details or making claims about results.

Short Positive Review Reply Template

Use this when the reviewer leaves only a sentence or a star rating with minimal detail.

Thank you for your kind review. We appreciate you choosing [Med Spa Name] and are grateful you took the time to share your feedback.

Mixed Feedback Reply Template

Use this when the review is mostly positive but includes one concern.

Thank you for sharing your feedback. We are glad parts of your visit were positive, and we also appreciate you letting us know where the experience could have been better. If you would like to discuss this directly, please contact us at [Phone or Email] so our team can learn more.

This keeps the response public-facing and calm while moving details to a private channel.

Negative Review Reply Template

Use this when a review is unhappy, emotional, or complicated.

Thank you for your feedback. We are sorry to hear that your experience did not meet expectations. Because privacy matters, we cannot discuss appointment details publicly, but we would appreciate the opportunity to learn more. Please contact us at [Phone or Email] so our team can review your concerns directly.

Neutral Review Reply Template

Use this for a three-star or brief review where the client does not give much context.

Thank you for taking the time to leave feedback. We appreciate the opportunity to serve you and are always looking for ways to improve the client experience at [Med Spa Name].

What To Avoid In Med Spa Review Replies

  • Do not mention private services, treatment plans, health details, or appointment specifics.
  • Do not argue with the reviewer or correct them point-by-point in public.
  • Do not promise specific beauty, wellness, medical, or financial outcomes.
  • Do not offer a discount or reward in exchange for changing a review.
  • Do not use the same robotic reply on every review.

Simple Weekly Reply Workflow

  1. Check new Google reviews once or twice per week.
  2. Tag each review as positive, short positive, mixed, neutral, or negative.
  3. Copy the closest template and personalize one safe sentence.
  4. Move sensitive or detailed conversations to phone or email.
  5. Track which reviews have been replied to so nothing sits unanswered.
