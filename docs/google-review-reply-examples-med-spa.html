5 star review reply examples

Example 1: warm and detailed

Thank you so much for sharing this. We are grateful you trusted [Med Spa Name] and so glad your visit felt like the experience we work hard to give every client.

Example 2: short and friendly

Thank you for the kind words. We appreciate you choosing [Med Spa Name] and look forward to welcoming you back.

Example 3: review mentions a staff member

Thank you for sharing this. Our team takes pride in giving every client a calm, attentive experience and we will pass along your kind feedback. We look forward to seeing you next time.

4 star review reply examples

Example 4: mostly happy, brief mention of a small issue

Thank you for taking the time to leave a review. We are glad most of your visit was a positive one, and we appreciate you flagging where the experience could have been smoother. If you would like to share more, please contact us at [Phone or Email].

Example 5: 4 stars with no comment

Thank you for your feedback. We appreciate you choosing [Med Spa Name] and are always looking for ways to improve the client experience.

3 star or neutral review reply examples

Example 6: vague middle-rating review

Thank you for taking the time to share your feedback. We appreciate the opportunity to serve you and would welcome any additional thoughts at [Phone or Email] so we can keep improving.

Example 7: mixed feedback with one clear concern

Thank you for your honest feedback. We are glad parts of your visit were positive, and we want to learn more about the part that was not. Please reach out to [Phone or Email] so our team can look into it directly.

1 to 2 star review reply examples

Example 8: general unhappy review

Thank you for sharing your experience. We are sorry your visit did not meet expectations. Because privacy matters, we cannot discuss appointment details publicly, but we would appreciate the opportunity to learn more. Please contact us at [Phone or Email].

Example 9: review mentions wait time or scheduling

Thank you for letting us know how your visit felt. A calm, on-time experience is what we aim for, and we are sorry yours did not match that. We would like to understand more. Please reach out at [Phone or Email].

Example 10: review mentions feeling rushed

Thank you for your honest feedback. Every client should leave feeling heard, not hurried, and we are sorry your visit did not reflect that. We would appreciate the chance to talk with you directly at [Phone or Email].

Example 11: review mentions cost or pricing

Thank you for sharing this feedback. We do our best to be transparent about pricing, and we are sorry your visit did not reflect that. Please contact us at [Phone or Email] so our team can review the details with you.

Example 12: review questions a result

Thank you for your honest feedback. Results can vary from person to person and we take it seriously when an experience does not meet expectations. Because privacy matters, we cannot discuss specifics publicly. Please contact us at [Phone or Email] so we can speak with you directly.

Example 13: review is emotional or upset

Thank you for taking the time to share this. We are sorry your visit was not the experience we wanted for you. Please reach out to [Phone or Email] so our team can listen and respond directly.

Example 14: review mentions hygiene or safety

Thank you for raising this. Cleanliness and safety are not negotiable in our med spa and concerns in this area go directly to leadership. Please contact us at [Phone or Email] so we can review your visit right away.

Example 15: review is short and harsh with no detail

Thank you for your feedback. We are sorry your visit did not meet expectations. If you are open to it, please contact us at [Phone or Email] so we can learn more.

How to personalize a template without breaking privacy

  • Echo a feeling the reviewer expressed, not a fact about their treatment.
  • Reference your values (calm, careful, professional) instead of their visit.
  • Thank them for the specific kind of feedback (warm, honest, detailed) instead of the content.

Frequently asked questions

How long should a med spa review reply be?

Two to four short sentences. Long replies read defensive.

Should I use the client's name?

You can use the name they signed with. Do not confirm any name beyond what they made public.

How fast should we reply?

Within 3 to 5 business days for positive reviews. Within 24 to 48 hours for negative reviews, after a short cool-off.
