Med spa review requests - After-treatment examples

Review request templates for after med spa appointments.

Use these SMS and email examples after facials, laser visits, injectable appointments, consultations, and follow-ups while asking for honest feedback instead of filtering only happy clients.

Timing matters for med spa review requests. A rushed same-day ask can feel transactional, but waiting weeks means the client may forget the details that made the visit feel good.

These templates are written for common med spa appointment types. Keep the language simple, avoid treatment-result promises, and use the same request for real clients rather than only asking people who seem thrilled.

No review gating: ask real clients for honest feedback. Do not pre-screen for satisfaction, offer rewards for reviews, ask for a specific star rating, or pressure anyone to post publicly.

Best Timing By Appointment Type

  • Facial or skin treatment: 24-48 hours after the visit, once the client has had a moment to settle back in.
  • Laser service: follow your clinic’s normal care timeline and avoid making claims about results in the review request.
  • Injectable appointment: avoid result-focused wording. Ask about the overall visit and team experience instead.
  • Consultation: send after the consult if the client had a complete experience with your staff, booking process, and recommendations.
  • Follow-up appointment: good moment to ask because the client has experienced more of the care process.

Facial Appointment Review Request SMS

Hi [First Name], thank you for visiting [Med Spa Name]. If you have a minute, we would appreciate your honest feedback about your experience with our team: [Google Review Link]

This works because it does not mention skin concerns, treatment details, or promised results.

Laser Appointment Review Request SMS

Hi [First Name], thank you for choosing [Med Spa Name]. Your feedback helps our team keep improving the client experience. If you are open to it, you can leave an honest Google review here: [Google Review Link]

Keep this general. Do not reference the body area, condition, or expected outcome in the message.

Injectable Appointment Review Request SMS

Hi [First Name], thank you for visiting [Med Spa Name]. We appreciate the opportunity to care for you. If you would like to share honest feedback about your visit, here is our Google review link: [Google Review Link]

For injectables, stay focused on the visit and team experience rather than appearance or results.

Consultation Follow-Up Review Request Email

Subject: Thank you for visiting [Med Spa Name] Hi [First Name], Thank you for coming in for your consultation with [Med Spa Name]. We appreciate the chance to answer your questions and help you understand your options. If you are willing to share honest feedback about your experience with our team, you can leave a Google review here: [Google Review Link] Thank you, [Team Member Name]

Follow-Up Appointment Review Request SMS

Hi [First Name], thank you for your recent follow-up with [Med Spa Name]. If you have a moment, we would be grateful for your honest feedback about your experience with our team: [Google Review Link]

Second Follow-Up Reminder

If your team sends one reminder, keep it light. The goal is a respectful nudge, not pressure.

Hi [First Name], quick note from [Med Spa Name]. If you still want to share feedback about your visit, our Google review link is here: [Google Review Link]. Thank you either way.

What To Avoid

  • Do not say “If you loved your results, leave us five stars.”
  • Do not ask only “happy” clients for reviews.
  • Do not mention private treatment details in texts, emails, or public replies.
  • Do not offer discounts, gifts, points, or perks for reviews.
  • Do not write messages that sound like medical, beauty, or financial outcome guarantees.
