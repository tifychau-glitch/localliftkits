What's inside

1. Spreadsheet workbook (.xlsx, opens in Sheets)

DashboardAuto-summary of signings this month, gross fees, miles driven, mileage deduction at the current IRS rate, unpaid invoices total, oldest unpaid, doc-type breakdown.
Signing LogOne row per signing. Date, signer, doc type, hiring company, fee, miles, scanback due, paid status. Dropdowns pre-filled.
MileagePulls from the signing log. Calculates deductible value at the 2026 IRS business rate ($0.70/mi). Monthly and YTD.
A/R by CompanyWho owes you what, days outstanding on the oldest unpaid, and auto-flags anything past 30 days.
Quarterly Tax EstimatorRuns gross fees + mileage deduction + flat expense estimate through SE tax + your income bracket. Outputs Q1-Q4 estimated payments. Not tax advice, just a starting point for your CPA conversation.
Scanback TrackerColor-coded urgency (red <24h, yellow <72h). Drops off the list once you mark it complete.
Companies DirectoryYour private notes on each hiring company. Payment terms, scheduler email, fee schedule, "always pays in 7", "uses SnapDocs", etc.
SettingsOne place to change your default fee, mileage rate, tax bracket, and business name. Everything else updates automatically.
