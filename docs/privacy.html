Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-05-14. Last updated 2026-05-14.

Local Lift Studio is a small one-person business that sells digital products (workbooks, checklists, templates) for service businesses and operational tooling. This page describes what information we collect, how we use it, and how to contact us with privacy questions.

Who runs this site

Local Lift Studio is operated by Tiffany Chau. Mailing address: 2100 Principal Row #405, Orlando, FL 32837. Contact email: localliftkits@gmail.com.

What information we collect

  • Purchase information. When you buy a product, our payment processors (Gumroad and, in some cases, Payhip via Stripe) collect your name, email address, billing information, and the products you purchased. We do not see or store full payment card numbers. We receive your name, email address, the product title, and the sale amount so we can fulfill the order and provide customer support.
  • Email list information. If you choose to sign up for our email list, Buttondown stores your email address until you unsubscribe. Unsubscribe links are included in every email.
  • Site analytics. We use GoatCounter for privacy-respecting site analytics. GoatCounter does not use cookies and does not collect personal information. It records anonymous page views, referrers, screen size, and rough geography at the country level.
  • White-label intake information. If you buy the Mobile Notary Operating System White-Label edition, Gumroad collects the business-name, brand-color, phone, email, and website values you enter at checkout so we can personalize the workbook before sending it to you.
  • Outreach correspondence. If we email you to ask a research question or share a resource, we use your publicly listed business email and we keep the thread on our own inbox at localliftkits@gmail.com. You can reply "no" or "unsubscribe" and we will not contact you again.

How we use it

  • To deliver the products you purchased.
  • To send you the personalized files you ordered (White-Label edition).
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To send emails you have explicitly opted into.
  • To understand which pages and resources are useful, in aggregate, via GoatCounter analytics.

How we share it

We do not sell your data and we do not share it with advertisers. The only third parties that handle your data are the operational tools listed above: Gumroad and Payhip for payments, Stripe for card processing within those platforms, Buttondown for email, GoatCounter for analytics, Gmail for support correspondence. Each of those has its own privacy policy and we recommend reviewing them.

About our Pinterest integration

We use Pinterest's API to post product-related pins and lead-magnet pins to our own Pinterest Business account. The Pinterest integration only writes to our Pinterest account; it does not read or store data from anyone else's Pinterest account or from visitors to our site. The Pinterest access token is stored locally and is not shared.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Tell you what information we have about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate information.
  • Delete your information (subject to legal record-keeping requirements for purchases).
  • Stop emailing you.

Send the request to localliftkits@gmail.com. We respond within a reasonable time and typically within seven days.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. GoatCounter is cookieless. If you make a purchase, the payment processor's checkout pages may set cookies governed by their own privacy policies.

Children

Local Lift Studio products are intended for business operators. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this page we will update the "last updated" date at the top. Material changes will also be announced on the Local Lift Studio home page.

Contact
